A Comprehensive Analysis of IT sector salaries and role




Overview:

It aims to analyze salary distribution across different IT roles, identify key roles and emerging trends, determine factors influencing salaries, compare salaries across industries, and perform skill-based analysis. The project will involve data collection, exploratory data analysis, visualizations, and reporting. The outcomes of the project will provide valuable insights for professionals and organizations in the IT sector.

One of the primary objectives of this project is to analyze the distribution of salaries across different IT roles. By examining the salary data, we can identify patterns, trends, and outliers in compensation packages. This analysis will provide an overall understanding of the landscape of salaries within the IT sector, including the range of salaries offered and any significant variations across roles.
